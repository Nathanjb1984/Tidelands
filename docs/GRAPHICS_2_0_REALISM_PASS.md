# Graphics 2.0 Realism Pass

Date: March 15, 2026  
Status: Active direction note

This pass shifts the shared Graphics 2.0 asset families away from icon-like placeholder sprites and toward more grounded coastal silhouettes.

## Direction

- Respect cultural grounding through believable material forms, posture, and silhouette rather than invented sacred decoration.
- Avoid fake crestwork, speculative ceremonial markings, or copied museum-style motifs in prototype assets.
- Favor cedar plank mass, woven-hat reads, paddles, posture, smoke, and shoreline staging over ornamental shorthand.
- Keep all assets readable at small portrait-mobile sizes.

## What changed

- Canoe hull silhouette now reads with more believable volume and prow/stern shape.
- Figures and witness sprites read more like clothed bodies than block icons.
- Longhouses emphasize roof mass, plank rhythm, and doorway depth.
- Pole families remain restrained and silhouette-first rather than decorative caricatures.

## Future higher-fidelity pass

- named-role character variants
- more witness posture variants
- dedicated close-up ceremonial figures
- FX sheets for ember, smoke, and surf overlays

## Guardrail

When in doubt, choose restraint and material truth over decorative invention.
